1. Who We Are

Reclaimed Group LLC ("Reclaimed Group," "we," "us," or "our") is a holding company operating multiple brands including Reclaimed Detailers and Reclaimed Co. This Privacy Policy applies to the website located at reclaimedgroup.com and all pages within it.

If you have questions about this policy, you can reach us at hello@reclaimedgroup.com.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you use our contact form, we collect:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your email address
  • The subject and content of your message

We only collect what you voluntarily submit. We do not require account creation to use this website.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, standard web server logs may automatically record:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring URL (how you found us)
  • Device type and operating system

This data is used only in aggregate form to understand how our site is used. We do not use it to identify individual visitors.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and messages
  • Notify you about brand launches if you've opted in (Reclaimed Shopping)
  • Improve the content and functionality of our website
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your information. We do not use it for automated decision-making or profiling. We do not send unsolicited marketing emails.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers

We use Formspree to process contact form submissions. Formspree may receive your name, email, and message content to facilitate delivery. Their privacy policy is available at formspree.io.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Reclaimed Group LLC, our users, or others.

Business Transfers

If Reclaimed Group LLC is acquired, merged, or its assets are transferred, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or a prominent notice on this site before your information is transferred or becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses a single piece of data stored in your browser's localStorage: your theme preference (light or dark mode). This is not a tracking cookie — it is a functional preference saved locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

We do not currently use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or any third-party tracking scripts. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated and you will be informed.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data.
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@reclaimedgroup.com. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We do not sell personal information and we do not discriminate against users who exercise their privacy rights.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Our website is served over HTTPS. Contact form data is transmitted securely via Formspree.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and you submit information at your own risk.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify users through a notice on the website. Your continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
