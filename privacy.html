1. Who We Are

Aioneo Group ("we," "us," or "our") is a holding company operating multiple brands including Aioneo Auto Detailing and Aioneo Co. This Privacy Policy applies to the website and all pages within it.

If you have questions about this policy, you can reach us at aioneogroup@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you use our contact form, we collect:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your email address
  • The subject and content of your message

We only collect what you voluntarily submit. We do not require account creation to use this website.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, standard web server logs may automatically record your IP address, browser type, pages visited, referring URL, and device type. This data is used only in aggregate form and is never used to identify individual visitors.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to respond to your inquiries, notify you about brand launches if you've opted in, improve the content and functionality of our website, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your information or send unsolicited marketing emails.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Service Providers

We use Formspree to process contact form submissions. Formspree may receive your name, email, and message content. Their privacy policy is available at formspree.io.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Aioneo Group, our users, or others.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses a single piece of data stored in your browser's localStorage: your theme preference (light or dark mode). This is never transmitted to our servers. We do not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party tracking scripts.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, email us at aioneogroup@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. California residents have additional rights under the CCPA — we do not sell personal information.

7. Data Security

Our website is served over HTTPS. Contact form data is transmitted securely via Formspree. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and you submit information at your own risk.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us
