Our Story

About Reclaimed Group

We're at the very beginning — and that's exactly where every great thing starts. This is the honest story of why Reclaimed Group LLC exists.

The Foundation

Where We're Coming From

Reclaimed Group LLC was founded with a simple conviction: that business can be built on something more than profit. Every brand we create, every service we offer, every product we put into the world — it all flows from a faith-rooted identity.

We're not pretending to be something we're not. Right now, Reclaimed Group is in its earliest stages. We don't have a long track record yet. What we do have is a clear vision, a firm foundation, and the belief that the best is still ahead.

The name "Reclaimed" isn't accidental. It speaks to transformation — things that were broken, restored. Things that were lost, found again. That's the heartbeat of everything we build.

"We are not yet who we will be — but we know exactly who we're becoming."

Transparency

Where We Stand Today

We believe honesty is part of excellence. So here it is plainly: Reclaimed Group LLC is a new company. Our first brand, Reclaimed Detailers, is actively being built and prepared to launch. Our second brand, Reclaimed Co., is in the design and planning phase.

We haven't completed our first job yet. We're not there yet — but we're close, and we're building it the right way: intentionally, honestly, and with longevity in mind from day one.

If you're reading this now, you're watching something get built from the ground up. We think that's something worth being part of.

Core Values

What We Stand On

Faith Over Fear

We build from a place of calling, not just strategy. Every decision is filtered through what we believe we're made to do.

Radical Honesty

We won't overstate where we are. We won't fake reviews, inflate numbers, or pretend to be further along than we are.

Long-Term Thinking

We're not chasing quick wins. Every brand we build is designed to still be standing — and still matter — decades from now.

Excellence in the Small Things

Before we can steward something large, we have to prove ourselves faithful with what's small. That standard starts now.
